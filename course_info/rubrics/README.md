# Rubrics
 

## Writing Workflow & Habits Portfolio

| Criterion                | Exemplary                                      | Satisfactory                       | Needs Improvement                 |
| ------------------------ | ---------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------- | --------------------------------- |
| Writing plan             | Clear, realistic, tailored to research context | Complete but generic               | Vague or impractical              |
| Tool use                 | Strategic selection and critical evaluation    | Tools used with limited reflection | Tools absent or poorly integrated |
| Metacognitive reflection | Insightful analysis of habits and barriers     | Mostly descriptive reflection      | Minimal or absent reflection      |
| Accountability           | Consistent writing logs                        | Inconsistent logging               | Little or no evidence             |

## Annotated Manuscript Drafts
| Criterion              | Exemplary                            | Satisfactory                | Needs Improvement          |
| ---------------------- | ------------------------------------ | --------------------------- | -------------------------- |
| Draft completeness     | Complete drafts submitted on time    | Minor gaps                  | Major sections missing     |
| Structural coherence   | Clear logic and signposting          | Uneven structure            | Disorganized               |
| Literature integration | Accurate synthesis of sources        | Patchy integration          | Poor or incorrect citation |
| Figures                | Clear, purposeful, publication-ready | Present but need refinement | Absent or unclear          |
| Revision quality       | Substantive revision across drafts   | Mostly surface edits        | Minimal revision           |


## Peer Review & Collaboration Portfolio
| Criterion            | Exemplary                          | Satisfactory        | Needs Improvement         |
| -------------------- | ---------------------------------- | ------------------- | ------------------------- |
| Peer review quality  | Specific, constructive, actionable | Generally helpful   | Vague or superficial      |
| Reviewer perspective | Journal- and audience-aware        | Partial awareness   | Reviewer role unclear     |
| Response to reviews  | Professional, justified, thorough  | Uneven responses    | Defensive or minimal      |
| Collaboration ethics | Clear articulation of norms        | Basic understanding | Misunderstandings evident |

## Final Manuscript & Reflection

| Criterion          | Exemplary                      | Satisfactory     | Needs Improvement     |
| ------------------ | ------------------------------ | ---------------- | --------------------- |
| Manuscript quality | Polished and submission-ready  | Solid but uneven | Not submission-ready  |
| Journal compliance | Fully compliant                | Minor issues     | Major noncompliance   |
| Cover letter       | Professional and persuasive    | Adequate         | Generic or incomplete |
| Reflective memo    | Insightful and forward-looking | Descriptive      | Superficial           |
