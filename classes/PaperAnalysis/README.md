#  Analysis of Published Paper

Analysing published papers that you enjoyed reading (and that are well-cited) to identify what makes them readable, engaging, and influential can be valuable for recognizing ways to develop (and improve upon) your own writing style. 
The goal (and challenge) is to be able to look past their scientific contributions and focus on the paper's _Structure_, _Flow_, and _Readability_.
