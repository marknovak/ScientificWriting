# Unfiltered Flow

Most writers stall not because they lack ideas, but because they edit as they generate — conflating two cognitively distinct processes. Unfiltered flow separates them. By writing under time pressure with no option to delete, you bypass the inner critic long enough to discover what you actually know, where your thinking is unclear, and what your manuscript needs next.

We'll use timed, high-velocity drafting activities to reduce perfectionism, increase fluency, and convert ideas into text.

---

### To do (_before class_)

Come mentally prepared for timed writing sprints!

Come with your manuscript open, a key figure ready to describe, and a rough sense of your central hypothesis — you'll use all three.

### Class structure

We'll proceed through a series of activities using [Squibler's Most Dangerous Writing App](https://www.squibler.io/dangerous-writing-prompt-app) to enforces continuous writing: if you stop typing for more than a few seconds, your text is erased!
