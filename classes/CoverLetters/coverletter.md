# Cover Letter Structure and Template

## Structure

### 1. Opening Paragraph — Submission and Contribution

Introduce the manuscript and its central contribution.

- *Submission statement:*
  State that you are submitting the manuscript and provide its full title.
- *Main finding:*
  Summarize the central finding or contribution of the paper.
- *Why it matters:*
  Explain the broader significance of the finding.



### 2. Contribution Paragraph — Summary of the Work

Provide a concise overview of how the research supports the main claim.

- *Model or conceptual framework:* 
  What approach did the study develop or use?
- *Empirical test:* 
  What data, experiments, or analyses support the conclusions?
- *Broader implications:* 
  What new insights emerge?



### 3. Journal Fit Paragraph — Relevance to the Journal

Explain why the manuscript is appropriate for the target journal.

- Why the topic aligns with the journal’s scope.
- Why the journal’s readership would find the research interesting.
- Any interdisciplinary relevance.


### 4. Compliance Paragraph — Editorial Declarations

Provide statements required by most journals.

- *Originality:*
  The manuscript has not been published and is not under consideration elsewhere.

- *Co-author approval:*
  All authors have read and approved the manuscript.

- *Data availability:*
  Data and code availability and repository information.

- *Prior review (if applicable):*
  Any previous friendly reviewers or recommendations.


### 5. Closing Paragraph — Final Framing

Conclude the letter and thank the editor.

- A brief statement expressing appreciation for consideration.
- Author signature and affiliation.

## Barebones Template

**Editor-in-Chief**  
*Journal Name*

Dear Dr. [Editor Last Name],

We are pleased to submit our manuscript entitled **"[Full Title of Manuscript]"** for consideration for publication in *[Journal Name]*.

Our study investigates **[brief statement of research question or objective]**. 
Using **[methods or approach]**, we demonstrate that **[key finding or contribution]**. 
These results contribute to **[broader significance or field relevance]**.

This manuscript fits the scope of *[Journal Name]* because **[1–2 sentences linking the paper to the journal’s audience, themes, or mission]**.

This manuscript is original work and has not been published previously nor is it currently under consideration for publication elsewhere. 
All authors have approved the manuscript and agree with its submission to *[Journal Name]*.

We have no conflicts of interest to declare.  
*[If applicable: Any relevant disclosures, data availability statements, or ethical approvals.]*

We appreciate your consideration of our manuscript and look forward to your reply.

Sincerely,

**[Author Name]**  
[Institution]  
[Email]
